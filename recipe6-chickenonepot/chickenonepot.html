Lemon & Herbs One Pot Chicken

Description

This Mediterranean-inspired dish combines the convenience of one-pan cooking with delightful flavours. Bright and fresh from the lemon marinade, onctuous from the slow cooking process and creamy rice. Enjoy as meal prep or with friends and family

Ingredients

  • Chicken Tighs or Breasts (thighs recommended)
  • Juice of 2 Lemons
  • 2 Tbsp - Greek Yogurt
  • 2 Tsp - Dijon Mustard
  • 2 Tbsp - Olive Oil
  • Garlic to Taste
  • Thyme, Oregano, Salt & Pepper
  • 1 - Brown Onion
  • 2 cups - Chicken Stock

Preparation

  • Mix your liquids, herbs and spices in a bowl
  • Add the chicken and toss. Marinate for 1 hour up to overnight
  • Pre-heat oven to 350F/180C (skip if using the slow cooker)
  • Chop your onion and garlic

Instructions

  1. Cook the chicken in the pot until brown, about 6 minutes per sides on medium high heat
  2. Remove chicken and set aside. Throw in the onion and some more herbs
  3. Sweat the onion and add garlic
  4. Add the rest of the chicken liquid, rice and mix until coated
  5. Add the chicken stock and bring to a simmer. Place the chicken on top. Cover
  6. Slow Cooker: 2hours. Oven: 20-25minutes @ 350F/180C
