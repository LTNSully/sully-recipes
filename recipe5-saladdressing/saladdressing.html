Salad Dressing

Description

This dressing is really a base recipe that can be used in so many different ways depending on what you find in your fridge of pantry. Kind of like a mother sauce.
It uses a few ingredients that will always be there and result in a creamy dressing that will go great with any salad. My favorite proteins to put in with it are chicken, bacon, and chickpeas.
You can change your quantities to obtain different textures or flavour.

Ingredients

  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Dijon Mustard/li>
  • Mayonnaise
  • Vinegar (I recommend Balsamic)
  • Garlic, crushed or powdered
  • Assorted herbs to taste
  • Salt + Pepper
  • Brine from pickled peppers (optional)

Preparation

  • Place all ingredients in a container
  • Mix until you obtain a homogenous dressing
  • Keep chill until you serve. Will last a few days in the fridge
